Weight Calculation Formulas

Precision Engineering Calculations

Material/Component Calculation Formula
Lead Pipe Calculation of S.S.Sheets, Circle, Pipes, Round Bar & Flat Bar
S.S. Sheets & Plates • Metric: Dia (mm) × Dia (mm) × Thick (mm) / 160 = Gms. Per PC
• Imperial: Dia (mm) × Dia (mm) × Thick (mm) × 0.00000063 = Kg. Per PC
S.S. Pipe • Weight per Meter: (O.D. - W.Thick) × W.Thick × 0.0248
• Alternate Formula: (O.D. - W.Thick) × W.Thick × 0.00758
S.S. Round Bar • Per Meter: Dia (mm) × Dia (mm) × 0.00623 = Wt. Per. Mtr.
• Per Feet: Dia (mm) × Dia (mm) × 0.0019 = Wt. Per. Feet.
S.S. Square Bar • Per Meter: Dia (mm) × Dia (mm) × 0.00788 = Wt. Per. Mtr
• Per Feet: Dia (mm) × Dia (mm) × 0.0024 = Wt.Per. Feet.
S.S. Hexagonal Bar • Per Meter: Dia (mm) × Dia (mm) × 0.00680 = Wt. Per.Mtr
• Per Feet: Width (mm) × Dia (mm) × 0.002072 = Wt. Per Feet
S.S. Flat Bar • Per Meter: Width (mm) × Thick (mm) × 0.00798 = Wt.Per Mtr.
• Per Feet: Width (mm) × Thick (mm) × 0.00243 = Wt.Per Feet.
Brass/Copper Pipe • Formula 1: (O.D. - Thick) × Thick × 0.0260 = Wt. Per Mtr.
• Formula 2: (O.D. - Wt) × Wt × 0.0345 = Wt. Per Mtr.
Aluminium Pipe (O.D. - Thick) × Thick × 0.0083 = Wt.Per. Mtr.
Aluminium Sheet Length (Mtr) × Width (Mtr) × Thick (mm) × 2.69 = Wt.Per PC
Weight of Conversion of Mtr to Feet
Wt of 1 Mtr. 3.2808 = Wt.Per Feet.
Barlow’s Formula
P = 2ST/D or S = DP/2t or D = 2st/p
Where: P = Bursting Pressure (psi) S = Tensile Strength T = Wall Thickness (inches) D = Outside diameter (inches)
